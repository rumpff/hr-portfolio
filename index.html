Unity Mania

I started this project with 2 goals: I wanted to create a rhythm game and I wanted to create a good looking UI.

I chose to make a VSGR (vertical scrolling rhythm game) because they aren't super complicated which leaves room for me to make the UI. I did some research on how Stepmania, an old but robust VSGR, handles its song charts. Turns out that they're saved very conveniently which allowed me to write a parser for those files, which saved me a lot of time that I didn't have to spend on making my own editor.

BWZ

In bowZ players only have a bow, unlimited arrows, they move very fast and get instantly spliced in half when hit by an arrow. Once a player gets 4 kills in a row that player receives a bounty, and gives double points when killed. The first player that scores 15 points wins.

Each bow has a special ability, which recharges over time and when the player makes a kill. Activating it will grant the player a special power for a limited time. For example, exploding arrows or teleportation.
